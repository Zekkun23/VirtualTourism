About Burj Khalifa
The tallest building in the world. Sometimes referred to as Burj Dubai, The Burj Khalifa is crowned as one of the most elite and highly coveted residential places in the world. Quite often, Dubai is recognised as a home to Burj Khalifa and other megastructures.
This towering skyscraper lies in the heart of Downtown, a district commonly referred to as the ‘Centre of Now‘. Standing tall as a notable landmark, it is also a top tourist attraction in Dubai. Be it a national holiday or an international event, Burj Khalifa is the centre of attraction. Most importantly, people flock here from around the globe to witness the exciting celebrations, fountain shows and fireworks.
