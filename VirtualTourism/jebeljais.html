About Jebel Jais
The Jebel Jais mountain peak is part of the spectacular cloud-piercing Hajar mountains. The range extends from Ras Al Khaimah on the eastern side of the United Arab Emirates to the Musandam Peninsula on the northeastern side of Oman.
