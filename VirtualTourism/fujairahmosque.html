About Sheikh Zayed Mosque
The Sheikh Zayed Mosque, Fujairah , is the main mosque in the Emirate of Fujairah, and the second largest in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) after the mosque with the same name in Abu Dhabi.
Named after the late Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan, founding father of the UAE, the mosque is a majestic edifice and an architectural work of art. The UAE is home to some of the most beautiful and picturesque mosques in the world, which attracts both residents and tourists from all over the world. One of the newest additions on the list is the Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Fujairah.
