About Ajman Etisalat Tower
A recent addition to the booming infrastructure of Ajman is its new landmark, Etisalat Tower. With mosque style architecture, this Etisalat Tower is a unique skyscraper in the Ajman skyline.
The basement contains the electricity, water, communication, and fire fighting equipment, the first and second floors is for customer services. The third floor has lectures theatres, and a social centre which serves various social activities. The other floors houses admin offices and mechanical and electronic equipment.
