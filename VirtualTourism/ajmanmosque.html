Sheikh Zayed Mosque Ajman

About Sheikh Zayed Mosque Ajman
Although the Emirate of Ajman has many religious sites, the Sheikh Zayed Ajman Mosque is the most famous religious site. This mosque has a rich architecture which is incredibly impressive with four minarets and a singular dome.
At a distance of 7 km from Ajman Bus Station, 21 km from Sharjah and 28 km from Dubai, Ajman Sheikh Zayed Mosque is a mosque located near Ajman University. It is one of the popular mosques in the emirate and among the prime places to visit in Ajman.
