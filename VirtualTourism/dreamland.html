About Dream Land
Dreamland aqua park is the largest family waterpark in the region with an area of more than 250,000 square metres. It's located on the picturesque coastline of Umm al-Quwain and its position makes it more accessible to tourists coming from the other Emirates.
An oasis of palm trees and green turf, one of the largest water parks in the region featuring : Over 30 thrilling rides and attractions, multiple restaurants to suit all tastes, a mini Zoo attraction for Kids, Tennis, Volley Ball and Basket Ball Courts, team building activities and conference room, pool bar and VIP lounge, jaccuzi, cabana rentals and overnight camping facilities and much more!
