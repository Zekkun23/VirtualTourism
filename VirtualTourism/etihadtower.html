About Ajman Etihad Tower
The towers offer 500,000 m2 of floor space, a 2500 m2 ballroom – the largest in Abu Dhabi, a curtain wall made from aluminium profiles and greyblue coated glass covering 135,000 m2, and a structural glazing façade spanning 6000 m2.
What is special about the Etihad Towers? Image result for etihad tower ajman A dramatic architectural statement on the Abu Dhabi Corniche, this five-tower complex welcomes residents and travellers to live, work, stay, shop and dine in one destination boasting stunning panoramic city and Arabian Gulf views.
